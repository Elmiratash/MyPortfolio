Biography

Elmira Tashvighi is a student at the UCLA extension coding program. She has a bachelor's degree in Interior design from California State University of Northridge. As a result of her lifetime passion for photography, Elmira had passed a few courses at UCLA in regards to photography and photo editing and has been involved with interior space photography and editing. Elmira was born and raised in Tehran and she moved to Los Angeles at the age of 23.
